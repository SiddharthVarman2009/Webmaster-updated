Cost and Tax rebate information

Geothermal Heat Pumps

The Residential Clean Energy Credit equals 30% of the costs of new, qualified clean energy property for a home in the United States installed anytime from 2022 through 2033. Costs 15,000 to 35,000 depending on amount of tons installed. Geothermal heat pumps save more money in the long run

Solar Panels

Great potential to save money. The tax rebate for solar panels is 30%. Costs around $25,000 without rebate. Cost range from 5,000 to 36,000 depending on the type and model. Price of solar panels is around $3 per watt but varies by state
